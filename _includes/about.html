Daniel Gologorsky, MD, MBA is a compassionate Ophthalmologist and fellowship-trained Retina specialist who takes care of patients with diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, retinal vascular occlusions, as well as other general eye pathology including glaucoma and dry eye. He sees patients in Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach.

A prolific reader and lecturer, Dr. Gologorsky has revived his residency blog (EyeBlog) to share his passion for ophthalmology and help his patients learn about the eye and eye disease.

This website does not replace the need to visit an eye doctor and receive professional medical advice. Please get in touch with any questions or blog post topic suggestions.
